Lumpiang Shanghai Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Â½ kilo ground pork
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix ground pork, carrot, onion, garlic, egg, soy sauce, salt, and pepper..
  2. Place a spoonful of the mixture on a lumpia wrapper and roll tightly. Seal with a little water. .
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat..
  4. Fry the lumpia until golden brown and crispy..
  5. Drain excess oil and serve with sweet chili sauce or ketchup
